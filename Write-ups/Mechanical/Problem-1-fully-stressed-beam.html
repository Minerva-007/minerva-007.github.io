Problem statement
A fully stressed non-prismatic simply supported rectangular beam of length 20m
and width 3m is subjected to a uniform load of 50kN/m. The mass of the beam is
included in the load. The material used has the properties E=210 GPa; YS=135
MPa. Applying a factor of safety of 1.69,
